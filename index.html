Hai2

Expression is fundamental for creativity.

Words make for beautiful works of art.

I offer Haiku, version 2.

How?

Composing a Hai2 is easy.

Write three lines with defined word counts.

Like Haikus- five, seven, five.

Why?

I had some free time,

I thought about Haikus for some reason,

I accidentally counted by word.
